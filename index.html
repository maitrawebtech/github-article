About the Founder

Ishan Maitra is the Founder & CEO of Maitra Web Tech, a company pioneering futuristic website design, AI-powered tools, and next-generation cloud solutions. At just 13, he has already made a mark in the tech world, being recognized as one of the youngest AI and Cloud Developers contributing to Google Cloud research projects. With a vision to merge imagination and technology, Ishan is building systems that not only push the boundaries of innovation but also empower the next generation to dream bigger, create smarter, and shape a future where technology serves as a true force of empowerment.

His vision: "To Develop to Empower."

Family & Support

Ishan’s journey has been deeply supported by his parents — Sanjay Maitra (Father) and Subhra Maitra (Mother). Their constant encouragement, guidance, and belief in his abilities have formed the foundation of his growth in both academics and technology. From nurturing his curiosity at a young age to motivating him to chase ambitious goals, they have played a pivotal role in shaping his vision, discipline, and determination to innovate. Their unwavering support continues to be the driving force behind his achievements and aspirations.
